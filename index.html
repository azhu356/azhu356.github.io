About Me

Hi there! My name is Alisha, and I am a Rutgers School of Engineering and Honors College student majoring in Biomedical Engineering and minoring in Statistics. I am enthusiastic about applying my analytical and creative mind to addressing current healthcare challenges. I hope to continue developing my technical skills through working on projects that promote innovation and collaboration. As an undergraduate research assistant, I am working on a project that aims to determine the relationship between biophysical properties of antimicrobial peptides and their drug efficacies. In my free time, you can find me painting or journaling!
